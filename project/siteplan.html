Site Plan

Name: Galaxy's Edge Guidebook

This is a simple name that clearly describes the site's purpose.

Purpose

This site is a guide to the attractions, experiences, food, and souvenirs available at Disney's Galaxy's Edge.

The site also features a form for users to sign up for the newsletter.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Where do I build a custom lightsaber or droid?
  • Scenario 2: What characters might I meet?
  • Scenario 3: What kind of food is available at each restaurant?
  • Scenario 4: What are the most popular rides, and how long are the wait times?

Color Schema

  • Background: Black
  • Text: White
  • Headings: Gold
  • Accents: Blue and Red

Typography

  • Headings: Orbitron
  • Body Text: Zen Kaku Gothic New
